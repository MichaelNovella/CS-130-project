This is a basic game of gomoku.

Win COnditions and play

The main way to win is to get five of your colored tiles in a row. In one of eight directions

Buttons

Around the playing square are different buttons that can be pushed to give a new board of varying size, a hint on where to place a piece, and a way to change the color scheme for the whole board.

Two textboxes

the two textboxes at the bottom are there for helping finding the hints.
